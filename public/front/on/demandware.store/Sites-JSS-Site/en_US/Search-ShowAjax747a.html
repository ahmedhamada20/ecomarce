Jack-o-Lanterns

Jack-o'-lantern pumpkins are a classic fall crop essential to traditional Halloween festivities, providing growers a favorable opportunity to extend the selling season. Many of our jack-o'-lantern varieties were bred by Johnny's or other New England regional collaborators. Johnny's offers a broad lineup of varieties selected for disease resistance — particularly to powdery mildew — vigor, and yield, as well as size, shape, weight, and color. To compare varietal features, see our Jack-o'-Lantern Pumpkin Comparison Chart.

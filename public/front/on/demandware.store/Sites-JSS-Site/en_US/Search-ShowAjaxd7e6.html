Strawberry

Nothing compares to the flavor of locally-grown, fresh strawberries, harvested at the peak of ripeness. We offer three main types of strawberries for sale: 1) June bearing (short day), 2) everbearing (day neutral), and 3) alpine. Choose from disease-free, dormant bare-root strawberry plants or strawberry seeds. Use our product filters to select strawberry varieties suited for container production or greenhouse growing and refer to our Strawberry Harvest Program to compare plant characteristics and harvest windows to extend your strawberry season.

Celery and Celeriac

Plant celery and celeriac for their edible stalks and roots, respectively. These closely related crops differ in texture but share the distinctive celery flavor. Johnny's offers adaptable celery and celeriac varieties that produce premium-quality yields under a range of common growing conditions. Both our celery and celeriac seeds are available with inert, NOP-compliant, size-8 pelleting. (See our Herbs section for Cutting Celery.)

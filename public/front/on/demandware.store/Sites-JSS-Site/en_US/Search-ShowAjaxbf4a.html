Slicing Cucumbers

Slicing cucumbers produce the largest fruits with the thickest skins, which gives them longer shelf life and makes them the best choice for shipping. Harvested at 7–8" in length, they have an average number of spines and bumps distributed over the dark-green skin of the fruit.

Burdock

Sow burdock seeds and cultivate the root for its sweet, mildly pungent flavor; high nutritional value; and curative medicinal properties. Among the hardiest of root vegetables, the long, tapered roots not only overwinter in the garden easily for spring digging but also store well, making it a versatile and distinctive crop for winter markets. Johnny's is pleased to offer a favorite Asian variety of this specialty root crop.

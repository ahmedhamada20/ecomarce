When trialing spinach varieties, Johnny's looks for the best combination of flavor, rapid growth, slowness to bolt, and disease resistance. Choose from a selection of varieties suited to sowing and harvest times throughout the growing season. For details, see our Spinach Planting Program. The flat smooth-leaved spinach varieties are traditional in the Western US and easiest to wash, reducing post-harvest labor.

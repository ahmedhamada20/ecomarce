Honeydew Melons

Johnny's offers a select group of honeydew varieties with earlier maturity, sweeter taste, more attractive appearance, and smaller size than traditional honeydews. Our honeydews are also easier to grow then regular honeydews, which require a long season and hot weather. The fruits have better tolerance to cracking and to cool weather, and have a high sugar content even under lower temperatures or cloudy conditions.

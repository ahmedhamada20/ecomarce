Seedless and Thin-skinned Cucumbers

Seedless and thin-skinned cucumbers are the best salad-quality cucumbers you can grow. Crisp, bitter-free, tender-skinned fruits require no peeling. Although suited to both indoor and outdoor growing, protected-culture methods for thin-skinned varieties will minimize damage from insect pressure and the elements. This category of cucumbers includes European or English cucumbers, Asian long cucumbers and Beit Alpha types as well as mini cucumbers (cocktail size).

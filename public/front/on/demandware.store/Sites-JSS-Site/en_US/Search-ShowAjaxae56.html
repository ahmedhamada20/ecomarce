Microgreens

Through years of trialing and selection, we've developed a line of microgreens noted for diverse flavors, colors, textures, and unique varieties. For growers, microgreens are increasingly popular because they're a year-round crop with fast turnaround, relatively easy to grow, and start-up costs are low. For consumers, microgreens are nutrient-dense, intensely flavorful, and simply beautiful—perfect for chefs, food enthusiasts, families, and the health conscious.

Standard Cauliflower

Standard cauliflower is a staple for farm stands and home gardens alike. We have carefully selected varieties in a range of colors, from the traditional white to the popular Cheddar orange, vivid green and purple, with varying degrees of heat and cold tolerance to suit conditions across the growing season and zones. An important quality in white cauliflowers is their wrapper leaves, which help to maintain the white color.

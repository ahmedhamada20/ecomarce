Pumpkins

Native to North America, pumpkins have been a significant fall harvest crop for over 5,000 years. Choose from award-winning Johnny's-bred pumpkin varieties, select open-pollinated heirlooms, and vigorous, disease-resistant hybrids. We offer organic and conventional seed in diverse colors, sizes, and shapes; whether you grow at a commercial scale or are selecting pumpkin seeds for planting in a home garden, we have a variety of options to meet your needs. Browse varieties for carving jack-o'-lanterns, baking pies, seed roasting and oil extraction, as well as ornamentals for traditional fall decorations. Use our convenient charts to compare days to maturity, average weights, edibility, plant spacing, vine length, disease resistance, and other variety-specific features. Bulk seed quantities available.

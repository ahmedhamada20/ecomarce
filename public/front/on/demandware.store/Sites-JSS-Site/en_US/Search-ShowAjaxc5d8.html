Blueberry

Blueberries, which are native to North America, can be divided into three major species: highbush, lowbush, and rabbiteye. Johnny's offers highbush blueberries as a collection of 18" dormant, bare-root plants of three different varieties: early, mid, and late-season, for an extended harvest. Multiple varieties are desirable in a single planting, as cross-pollination produces a better crop of berries; see our Blueberry Harvest Program for estimated harvest dates.

Lollo Lettuce

Lollo lettuce varieties have broad leaves in green or red tones, with heavily curled margins. Generally grown as full-size heads for garnish, lollo lettuce varieties can also be used for baby leaf production. The leaves of the heads do not close tightly but form an open rosette, allowing them to be picked as needed for salads without harvesting the whole plant.

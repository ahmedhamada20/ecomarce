Summer Squash

In contrast to winter squash, summer squash is harvested when it is tender and comparatively small, making it ideal for easy summer baking, sauteing, and grilling. Our summer squash varieties vary in color from dark to palest green and boldest yellow, with variations in stripes, solids, and shapes, from perfect slender cylinders to the disk-shaped patty pans.

Fresh Shell Beans

One of those simple, yet gourmet items rarely seen in markets, the rewards of fresh shell beans usually belong to those who grow their own. Prepared like lima beans, fresh shell beans are much easier to grow and shell. Easily frozen or canned for winter use. Harvest pods when beans are plump.

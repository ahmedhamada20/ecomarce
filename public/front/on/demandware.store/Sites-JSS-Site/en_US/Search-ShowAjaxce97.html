Sweet Peppers

Enjoy a large selection of organic sweet peppers from Johnny's, including many of our own breeding. Small, bite-size snack peppers or larger, multiuse peppers make up this group. In addition to snack peppers, choose from a number of sweet specialties — banana, cubanelle, and frying peppers, to name a few. Colors range from shades of red and orange to yellow, and green, with smooth or wrinkled skin. Eat these peppers fresh, fried, or pickled.

Pole Beans

Pole beans are an excellent choice for those with limited garden space. They offer faster picking, much higher yields, longer harvest periods, and a delicious flavor superior to even the best bush beans. We have included round-pod, flat-pod, and yard-long varieties in our selection of pole beans.

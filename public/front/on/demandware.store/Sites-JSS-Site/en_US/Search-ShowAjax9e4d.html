Cucumbers

Johnny's cucumber varieties are selected for flavor, disease resistance, productivity, and appearance, as demonstrated in trialing conducted both in the field and under protected culture. Cucumbers are also one of the crops most widely grown in hydroponic systems. Choose from slicing, pickling, seedless and thin-skinned, specialty, and greenhouse performers, in a range of seed type options, including treated and certified-organic.

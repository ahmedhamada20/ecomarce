Snap Peas

Snap peas are the round-pod type of edible-pod peas, distinct from snow peas, which have flat pods. Pick snap peas when both the peas and the pods are plump and "snap" like a fresh green bean. Eat the pods and peas together like snow peas. Refer to our comparison chart to compare snap peas with shelling pea types.

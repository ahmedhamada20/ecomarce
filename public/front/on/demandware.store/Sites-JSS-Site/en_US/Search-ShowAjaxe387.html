Sorrel

Sorrel's tangy leaves add a bright, lemony flavor to soups, salads, and sauces. Choose an all-green or red-veined variety to grow full size or harvest at baby-leaf stage. Cultivated as a cool-weather crop. When grown as a perennial, divide the plants every 2 years.

Main Crop Carrots

Main-crop carrot varieties can be planted in spring to mid summer, for summer to fall harvest. Our selection includes varieties chosen for good flavor even in warm conditions and types and varieties that will not bolt too early. To compare size and shape of our main crop carrot varieties, see our Main Crop & Storage Carrot Typology Chart.

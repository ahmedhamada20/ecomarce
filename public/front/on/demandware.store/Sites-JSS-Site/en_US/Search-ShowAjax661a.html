Corn

Johnny's offers a line of corn varieties selected for superior performance, yield, quality, and flavor, to suit the needs of discerning home gardeners and small commercial growers. Our line of open-pollinated, heirloom, and traditionally bred fresh-eating varieties includes old-fashioned sweet corn, super sweet corn, SuperSeedWare corn, normal, sugary enhanced, and synergistic sweet types with white, yellow, and bicolor kernels, with varying maturity dates and disease resistance packages. Our dry corns include flour and dent corns, ornamental and broom corns, and popcorns in a range of sizes and colors. Available in organic and conventional seed forms.

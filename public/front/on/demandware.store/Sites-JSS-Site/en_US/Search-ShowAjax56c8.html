Mini Eggplants

With their dark purple, white or striped skin, our mini eggplants grow to no more than 4 inches in length, developing on high-yielding compact plants ideal for container growing. These baby eggplants are true babies with a convenient, small size and non-bitter flavor that makes them perfect for single-serve dishes of all types. They are excellent grilled, roasted, stuffed, or thinly sliced over pizza.
